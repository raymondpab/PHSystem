IDEA

Help the Barangay Health Workers gather and store data in an easy and convenient way.

  • Children's Records
  • Pregnant Women
  • Senior Citizens
  • PWDs

    • DESIGN

    A system that can help the Barangay Health Workers to transform the manual process of gathering data into computerized process. Wherein, it will be easier for them to monitor the Poverty and Health issues in the barangay.

    DEVELOP

    We will design, develop and implement a database system that organizes the records of poverty and health status in the barangay. The monitoring system will replace the existing manual process. This will accumulate information in a more efficient and faster way.
